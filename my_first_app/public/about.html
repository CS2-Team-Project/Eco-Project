    Welcome to Concept


    Welcome to Concept where quality craftsmanship, sustainable fashion, and modern style combine all in one. Learn more about our values below.

    Made in the UK

    At Concept we take pride in keeping the design and production of our merchandise at home in the UK. We keep our production and manufacturing local so we can ensure exceptional quality, originality, and cater to the values of our brand. This action is an integral part of Concept. We also believe it is crucial to the quality of our products. By keeping our production and manufacturing local, not only do we ensure strong attention to detail and uphold a reputation for fashion for the country, but we also serve as a benefactor for UK-based businesses.

    Our Mission

    At Concept, our purpose is straightforward and simple, however, we want to have a huge impact. We don't just want to provide exceptional coats and jackets that offer comfort, warmth, and exceptional style. We also want to revolutionize fashion in modern culture in a way where people can express themselves and stand out amongst others. For us, coats and jackets are more than just what you wear. It is a statement. It is a companion throughout life's journeys.

    Sustainability & Commitment

    In our mission to help preserve the planet and fight back against climate change, one of our highest priorities is to produce the most sustainable jackets that provide comfort and exceptional style in order to keep our people's best interests at heart. Our cotton is 100% organic and we also use recycled materials that have low impact on the environment by minimizing waste and ensuring less carbon footprint. To achieve our goals means that we align ourselves with the right environmental organisations to set an example and leave behind an environmental legacy.

    Certifications


    Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS)

    We are committed to GOTS certified organic materials which means our fabrics are responsible for the environment and society. The fabrics we use in production processes for our jackets are low on water usage void of any harmful chemicals. Choosing materials certified by GOTS allows us to cut down our ecological footprint while providing top-tier fashionable apparel.

    Bluesign速 Approved

    We use Bluesign速 approved fabrics to help produce our jackets and coats, This helps us reduce harmful chemicals to help preserve the environment. This guarantees that every process from dyeing to finishing is Bluesign certified to be sustainable by avoiding water pollution and hazardous waste. We use Bluesign速 certified textiles to reduce our environmental footprint and offer durable, performance driven outerwear that is consistent with our mission of a better planet.

    Responsible Down Standard (RDS)

    For our puffer and insulated jackets, 100% of the down used are ethically sourced and RDS certified. RDS certification is a standard code of practice which incentivizes the down and feather industry. This helps us guarantee the humane treatment of wildlife animals such as ducks and geese as all feathers are ethically sourced in adherance to animal welfare guidelines.
